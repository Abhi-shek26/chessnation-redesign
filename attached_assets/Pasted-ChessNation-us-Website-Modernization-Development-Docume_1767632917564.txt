1. Purpose of This Document
This document serves as the developer-ready specification for modernizing the ChessNation.us website. It consolidates mission, positioning, page structure, content requirements, and functional expectations so development can proceed immediately with minimal ambiguity.
Primary objectives: - Clearly position ChessNation as a training-first chess institution with a strong technology backbone - Support tournaments, training, franchise growth, and ecosystem initiatives - Deliver a modern, mobile-first, fast, and professional website
________________________________________
2. Mission & Positioning
2.1 Primary Mission Statement
ChessNation is focused on training and building tomorrow’s chess talents by combining world-class coaching, competitive exposure, and cutting-edge chess technology.
2.2 Expanded Mission
At ChessNation, our mission is to identify, train, and develop the next generation of chess talent through structured training programs, competitive pathways, and AI-powered chess technologies.
We believe modern chess excellence requires more than coaching—it requires systems, exposure, and community. ChessNation bridges all of these into one unified ecosystem.
2.3 Core Focus Pillars
1.	Training Tomorrow’s Chess Talent
2.	Technology for Chess (ChessEvents.AI)
3.	Chess Ecosystem & Endowment Building
2.4 Tagline (Primary)
Master the Royal Game – Conquer the World
Alternate (optional, secondary use): - Elite training meets chess technology - We build players. We build platforms. We build the future of chess - Train • Compete • Build
